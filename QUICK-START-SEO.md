## 🚨 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. ❌ Keyword stuffing (using keywords too much)
2. ❌ Duplicate content across pages
3. ❌ Slow page load times (>3 seconds)
4. ❌ Missing alt text on images
5. ❌ No mobile optimization
6. ❌ Broken internal/external links
7. ❌ Missing meta descriptions
8. ❌ Thin content (<300 words)
9. ❌ No internal linking
10. ❌ Ignoring analytics data

## 💡 QUICK SEO TIPS

### Title Tags
✅ Good: "Digital Pathology Platform | AI Diagnostics | Turocrates.ai"
❌ Bad: "Home - Turocrates"

### Meta Descriptions
✅ Good: "Turocrates.ai provides AI-powered digital pathology platform with whole slide imaging and pathology education tools for precision diagnostics."
❌ Bad: "Welcome to our website"

### Headings
✅ Good: "AI-Powered Digital Pathology Platform"
❌ Bad: "Welcome"

### Image Alt Text
✅ Good: "Digital pathology platform interface showing whole slide imaging"
❌ Bad: "image1.jpg"

### URLs
✅ Good: /blog/digital-pathology-guide
❌ Bad: /blog/post123
