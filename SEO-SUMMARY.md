## 💡 KEY SEO TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Content Strategy
1. **Publish Consistently**: 2-3 blog posts per week
2. **Focus on Quality**: 1500+ words, well-researched
3. **Target Long-Tail Keywords**: Less competition, higher conversion
4. **Answer Questions**: Use FAQ format for voice search
5. **Update Old Content**: Keep content fresh and relevant

### Link Building
1. **Quality Over Quantity**: Focus on authoritative sites
2. **Diverse Link Profile**: Mix of guest posts, directories, partnerships
3. **Natural Anchor Text**: Avoid over-optimization
4. **Build Relationships**: Network with industry leaders
5. **Create Linkable Assets**: Resources, tools, research

### Technical SEO
1. **Monitor Core Web Vitals**: Keep scores in green
2. **Fix Errors Quickly**: Use Search Console to identify issues
3. **Optimize for Mobile**: Mobile-first indexing is priority
4. **Improve Page Speed**: Target <3 second load times
5. **Use Structured Data**: Help search engines understand content
