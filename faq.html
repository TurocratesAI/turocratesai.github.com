Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Turocrates.ai digital pathology platform.

Platform & Technology

What is Turocrates.ai?
Turocrates.ai is a comprehensive digital pathology platform that combines AI-powered diagnostics with whole slide imaging, pathology education tools, and a vendor-neutral platform for pathology workflows.
What digital pathology formats do you support?
Our platform supports all major whole slide image formats including SVS, NDPI, MRXS, SCN, and other standard WSI formats. We're scanner and vendor neutral.

AI & Diagnostics

How accurate is your AI for cancer detection?
Our clinical AI suite for breast cancer diagnostics achieves within 5% accuracy compared to expert pathologist ground truth for ER, PR, and KI67 scoring.
Can I use my existing microscope with your platform?
Yes! Our platform is designed to work with any microscope camera. We provide real-time AI analysis directly through your existing microscope setup.

Education & Training

What is TuroEducate?
TuroEducate is our comprehensive pathology education platform featuring interactive whole slide images, case studies, quizzes, and collaborative learning tools for pathology education.

Technical & Integration

How do I integrate with my existing systems?
Our platform offers REST APIs, DICOM support, and HL7/FHIR integration for seamless EMR/LIS system integration.
